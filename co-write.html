INT. BAKER'S PHANTOM - NIGHT
Inside a dimly lit bakery, the shelves are lined with creepy, malformed bread — each loaf has an eerie face carved into it. A thick fog filled with the scent of stale flour permeates the room. A little girl, Mary (8), with wild curls and a puppet clutched to her chest, wanders through the bakery in a daze.
